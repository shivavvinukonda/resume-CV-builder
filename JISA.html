INFORMATION SECURITY ANALYST

An information security analyst is a professional who is responsible for protecting an organization's computer systems and networks from cyber threats and attacks. This includes implementing and managing security measures such as firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and security protocols to safeguard sensitive information and prevent unauthorized access.
